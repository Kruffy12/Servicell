Service Policies

Last Updated: April 3, 2026

At ServiCell, we believe in transparent repairs. These policies ensure we provide the best care for your technology and keep our prices fair for everyone in Belize City.

1 Before the Repair

Backup Your Data

Repairs can be unpredictable, especially with hardware logic board issues. Please back up your device (iCloud, Google Drive, or PC) before drop-off. ServiCell is not liable for data loss, though we prioritize data safety in every repair.

2 During the Repair

Hidden Damage

Cracked screens can hide deeper motherboard or impact damage. If we discover additional issues upon opening the device, we will contact you immediately before proceeding.

Water Resistance

Opening a factory-sealed device breaks the original waterproof bond. While we use high-grade industrial adhesives to reseal your phone, we cannot guarantee original water-resistance ratings. Keep repaired devices away from water.

Customer-Supplied Parts

If you bring your own screen or battery for us to install:

  • Labor Only: You are paying for the technical service. We do not warranty the quality or lifespan of parts we didn't source. This means ServiCell is responsible only for the installation work, not the part itself.
  • Defective Parts: If your part is a "lemon" (a defective product that fails shortly after purchase due to manufacturing flaws), you must handle the return or warranty claim with your original seller. ServiCell cannot process returns on third-party parts.
  • Double Labor: If a part you supplied fails and needs replacement, a second labor fee will apply for the removal and reinstallation of a new part. We recommend using ServiCell-sourced parts to avoid additional costs.
ServiCell Pro-Tip: Want a full peace-of-mind guarantee? Buy your parts directly from us. When we provide both the part and the labor, we take 100% responsibility for the fix!
3 After the Repair

The 3-Day Test Drive

We want you to be sure! You have a 3-day window from the time of pickup to report any issues with the installed part. If it’s not working perfectly, bring it back and we’ll make it right immediately.

4 Pickup & Abandonment

90-Day Storage Rule

We value your tech, but we have limited space. Devices not picked up within 90 days of the "Ready for Pickup" notification will be considered abandoned and may be sold or recycled to recover repair costs.

Track Your Repair: Use our Repair Tracker to monitor your device status and receive timely pickup reminders.

5 Agreement

By leaving your device at ServiCell (#7 Douglas Jones Street), you agree to the terms listed above. We appreciate your trust in our technicians!
